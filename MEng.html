About
My domain of expertise in mechanical engineering is structural simulation. I am an expert in the Finte Element Method (FEM), having both programmed various solvers as well as used nummerous commerical software. I currently have more that six years of industrial experiance in this field and four years of academic experience before it. In this section of my website I list the programs I have used, the subareas of expertise and some milestone industrial projects. In addition, you will find some information on lectures/seminars I have given on simulation topics and my publications.
